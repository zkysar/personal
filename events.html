Japanese Language Exchange

A vibrant community dedicated to Japanese language learning and cultural exchange. Our members range from complete beginners to native speakers, creating an inclusive environment for everyone interested in Japanese language and culture.

We organize regular meetups where participants can practice their Japanese conversation skills, make new friends, and learn about Japanese culture in a welcoming atmosphere.

Almost 700 members as of June 2025

SF Frens Zine Events

SF Frens is a supportive community for creative growth based in San Francisco. We produce a biannual zine featuring short stories, poetry, art, and experimental content like crosswords, weather data visualizations, and maps.

Each zine edition is celebrated with a launch party that doubles as an open mic event, creating opportunities for community expression and connection. With four editions published so far, we continue to grow and welcome new contributors to our creative community.

Four editions published as of June 2025
